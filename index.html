About Me

About 3 years back, when I was pregnant, I faced a lot of issues because I live in a nuclear family where both my husband and I were working.

Through this experience came the idea of making life of working women (and women in general) a bit easy with this concept of an online portal for vegetables and fruits.

Fruit Network is launched to help these people, to help people with busy lifestyles. I wanted to help women improve the quality of their lives!
